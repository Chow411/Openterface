Product Features

Plug-and-Play

Enables rapid use without software installed or configurations on the target computer.

Stable & Fast Control

Headless control via HDMI and emulated keyboard/mouse, without network concern.

A Switchable USB-A Port

Toggles USB usage between host and target, ideal for sharing a USB stick without the need to replug.

BIOS-Level Access

Enables direct access to the target device's BIOS and firmware management.

No Residue

Ensures a tidy usage experience by not leaving any software remnants on the target computer.

Ultimate Portability

Its lightweight design makes it easy to carry, small enough to fit into your pocket.
